My name is Stefania Bouman and I live in Bucharest, Romania. I graduated in Computer Science for Businesses at the Romanian-American University back in 2019.

Though I chose other jobs (which you can check in my CV) at first, there was always a part of me nagging me towards this field of interest. So now, after wondering about, you can say the wayward daughter has finally decided to come and do what she was always destined to !

Working in other fields has given me the opportunity to develop myself as a very versatile personae, so now I am ready to apply all I got in the next chapter of my life.

Why the interest in Graphic and Web Design? To be fair, I was never good at drawing - or doing something too complicated with my bare hands altogether - but I had so many ideas which I was dying to manifest into reality. So when I found out about this field I just HAD to try it! And so I fell in love with the possibilities.

What are my plans for the near future? Well, in 2021 I finished the Web Design course at Link Academy, so by the end of the spring in 2022 I will take my Adobe Certification Exam in Illustrator. I also have plans for the Dreamweaver Professional Certification, but one step at a time .
